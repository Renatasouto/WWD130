Overview

Purpose

The world is technological now. Software development careers are promising.
Many people need help knowing where to start. This site focuses on helping beginners
learn more about careers, the job market, tips on study materials and forums.

Audience

My target audience is software development beginners and people who want to know more about the coding world.
